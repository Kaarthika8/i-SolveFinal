Hack The Bangalore Traffic

In the vibrant city of Bangalore, traffic congestion has become a pressing challenge that affects daily commuters and the overall quality of life. To address this issue, we present the i-SOLVE Hackathon, a dynamic platform aimed at generating innovative solutions for managing Bangalore's traffic woes. Participants will have the opportunity to leverage technology, data analytics, and smart solutions to develop applications that optimize traffic flow, enhance public transport efficiency, and promote sustainable commuting practices.
